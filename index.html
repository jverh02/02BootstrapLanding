Anthony J. Natalizio Center (AJN)

The newly renovated AJN Center boasts 17,665 square feet that can be divided and arranged for a variety of event styles. Capacity: 1,376 (theater seating), 608 (banquet seating), 225 (classroom seating)

Richard T. Anderson (RTA) Education Center

The RTA Center’s 4,500 square feet can be divided and arranged for a variety of event styles, plus two classroom-sized breakout rooms are available just across the hall for additional space. Capacity: 420 (theater seating), 240 (banquet seating), 160 (classroom seating)

Building Q

Training rooms with different sizes and configurations are available for customized corporate training, seminars and special events. Capacity: 30 per room (three rooms available)

Lecture Halls

The WCTC campus features several lecture halls, ideal for educational events and presentations. Capacity: 105 (half room), 210 (whole room)

The Classic Room

Find delectable dining in the Classic Room, a vibrant student training restaurant available to rent for private events.


Other facilities are available upon request. Please call for more information.

Catering

If you're hosting an event at WCTC, our in-house provider, Aladdin , can provide a variety of dining options for your event. This includes continental and hot breakfasts; reception trays; box lunches featuring traditional sandwiches, wraps or salad options; full lunch buffets; pizza; desserts; beverage service and much more. Aladdin is the preferred provider for events held on campus.
